--- APP DESCRIPTION ---

Field Log Pro is the fastest way for landscaping professionals and pesticide applicators to log chemical applications in the field. Complete a full application log in under 30 seconds with automatic GPS coordinates and real-time weather capture.

Built for technicians working outdoors, the glove-friendly interface makes it easy to log chemicals used, target pests, application amounts, and attach required label photos right from the job site.

Generate state-compliant PDF reports for California (DPR), Florida (DACS), and Texas (TDA) regulations with a single tap. Customers automatically receive email notifications with application details and safety information.

Key Features:
- Log pesticide applications in under 30 seconds
- Automatic GPS location and weather auto-fill
- Searchable database of chemicals with EPA registration numbers
- Photo capture for product labels, before and after treatment
- State-compliant PDF report generation (CA, FL, TX)
- Automated customer email notifications
- Complete audit trail for regulatory compliance
- Multi-user company accounts with role-based access
- Application history with date, customer, and chemical filtering
